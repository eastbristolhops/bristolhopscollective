Jon Wheway
He/Him
  • Years with BHC: Jon has been involved with the project since 2017
  • Position: Chairperson
  • Hop varieties: Prima Donna, Cascade and Centennial
  • Hobbies: Allotment, Beekeeping, Software develoment, Music and Beer
  • Contact: bristolhopscollective@gmail.com
With a passion for mixing technology and food, Jon is the maintenance manager at LettUs Grow, an impact-led indoor farming company specialising in Aeroponic technology. He believes quality nutritious food is a right and should be available to everyone, so bringing it closer to source, reducing waste and the carbon footprint is of utmost importance to protect the environment.

When not at work or organising Bristol Hops Collective, Jon can be found down his allotment, tending his 5 bee colonies or socialising with friends sampling good quality beer.

Apart from spending his time at allotments and organising Bristol Hops Collective, Guy also can often be found at a wide variety of genre-crossing gigs and helping to organise Bristol Seed Swap.
