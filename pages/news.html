Something To Shout About

21/10/2023

This years collaborative brew is with New Bristol Brewery. Its been made and is now in the fermentation vessel. All the hops supplied by the collective went into the brew kettle so we are all really excited to see how it will turn out. Dates for the launch party are TBC so keep checking back for more info.


20/08/2023

Due to the closing of Dawkins Ales, Team BHC have been in talks with breweries across Bristol to find a new brewing partner to brew the 2023 Bristol Hops Collective brew. We are delighted to announce that New Bristol Brewery, will be stepping in. We certainly look forward to what amazing beer the team is going to produce.
