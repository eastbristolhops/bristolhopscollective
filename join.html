Join Us

Welcome to Bristol Hops Collective, to pre-order your hop plant and become a part of the collective simply fill out the form below, and a member of our team will be in touch with the next steps on how to pay.

Once you sign up, Bristol Hops Collective will order your hop plants, provide you with a growing kit and all you need to know to grow a hop plant. We’ll then organise collective harvesting, brewing and celebratory drinking events in the Autumn – where we will be given a barrel or two of the collective beer to enjoy. The rest will be supplied to pubs around Bristol so you can go order a pint of your own beer at your local.

When the rhyzomes have been delivered usually around the begining of March. We will then provide your selected hop kit, which will include almost everything you will need to get started. Kits contain the hop rhizome, mulch, twine and a hop peg.
After Planting you can trail the hop bines on a freestanding trellis, up and around a lean-to, walls, etc. You can grow the hops in a pot (minimum of 40-litre volume, and at least 30 cm deep) or straight in the ground.
Then come together as we all harvest at the legendary Cropathon/weigh-in ready for the beer to be brewed at the selected brewery. Most importantly come back to celebrate and drink the locally grown truly bristolian beer.

If you are new to growing thats fine simply go and check out our HopsWiki for tips on growing your hops if you need them. Failing that join our Facebook group where seasoned growers can offer help and advice.

Kits are £20 each and additional rhizome's £10 each.

Remember, hops are herbaceous perennial, so once you have planted the hop rhizome it should grow back each year, provided it is protected from frosts in the winter and droughts in the summer. This is a one-off investment - unless you want to order additional plants next year!

** Dont forget to place your order and pay by the 1st of March as otherwise we cannot guarantee you a plant. **

Thanks for getting involved!

Unsure of what kit to Select?

We have 2 kits on offer. Dwarf hops and standard (more vigorous) hops. Reghardless of type, they are both the same price £20 per kit and £10 per additional rhyzome. If you have limited growing space then we recommend ordering a dwarf which will grow to about 2-3m. If you have some more space, the more vigorous hop varieties will have bines that grow to around 5m which you’ll need to trail around the place a bit more.
