Join Us

Welcome to Bristol Hops Collective. To pre-order your hop plant and become a part of the collective, fill out the form below and a team member will be in touch.

Once ordered, Bristol Hops Collective will acquire your plant and the growing kit and will arrange collection.
We'll then be on call during the growing season for advice if needed. We will also liaise with a local brewery and arrange social events in autumn for a weigh-in and a drink-up to launch the new beer. The beer will be supplied to pubs around Bristol so that you can order a pint at your local. It will also be available in cans to enjoy at home.

When the rhizomes have been delivered, usually around the beginning of March, we will arrange for you to collect your plant and kit. Kits include mulch, twine and a hop peg. Hop plants perform best if planted directly in the soil, but dwarfing varieties can successfully be grown in large pots. As a bining plant, they will need a structure to grow up. Our HopsWiki is packed full of tips on growing your hop plant. We also have an active Facebook group where seasoned growers can offer help and advice.

Kits are £20 each, with additional rhizomes priced at £10 each.

Remember, hops are hardy herbaceous perennials, so once you have planted the rhizome, it should grow back each year. They will also need minimal care once established.

** Dont forget to place your order and pay by the 1st of March as otherwise, we cannot guarantee you a plant. **

Unsure of what kit to Select?

We have 2 packs on offer. Dwarf hops and standard (more vigorous) hops. Reghardless of type, they are both the same price £20 per pack and £10 for each additional rhyzome. If you have limited growing space then we recommend ordering a dwarf variety which will grow to about 2-3m. If you have some more space, the more vigorous hop varieties will have bines that grow to around 5m which you’ll need to trail around the place a bit more.
